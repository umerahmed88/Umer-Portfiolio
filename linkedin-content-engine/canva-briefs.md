# Canva Briefs for LinkedIn Visuals

Create three reusable Canva templates so posts look consistent without taking too much time.

## Template 1: Market Insight Card

- Size: 1200 x 1200 px
- Use for: one strong market insight or quote
- Layout:
  - Large headline
  - Small subheading
  - Your name / profile handle
  - Small KSA/GCC category label
- Example headline: `Saudi Arabia is building a new business operating system.`

## Template 2: Carousel

- Size: 1080 x 1080 px
- Export as PDF for LinkedIn carousel upload
- Use for: educational posts and roundups
- Slide structure:
  1. Strong cover hook
  2. Context
  3. Trend 1
  4. Trend 2
  5. Trend 3
  6. Takeaway
  7. Final question / CTA

## Template 3: Weekly Roundup

- Size: 1200 x 1200 px
- Use for: Saturday recap posts
- Layout:
  - Title: `5 GCC updates to watch this week`
  - Numbered list area
  - Footer: `Follow for KSA/GCC business and AI insights`

## Visual Style

- Palette: desert sand, deep charcoal, warm gold, white text
- Font style: modern sans-serif, high contrast, readable on mobile
- Avoid: overdecorated graphics, too much text, fake statistics
