# ChatGPT Prompts for KSA / Middle East LinkedIn Growth

Use these prompts after collecting topics from Google Alerts, Feedly, or the RSS collector.

## 1. Convert a news item into LinkedIn content

```text
You are my LinkedIn content strategist for Saudi Arabia, UAE, and the Middle East.

My goal:
Grow my LinkedIn personal brand among founders, business owners, tech professionals, recruiters, and decision-makers in KSA and the GCC.

Topic/article:
[Paste article title, summary, and URL here]

Create:
1. A simple summary of the trend.
2. Why this matters in KSA/GCC.
3. 5 strong LinkedIn hooks.
4. 1 short post under 180 words.
5. 1 longer storytelling version.
6. 1 opinion-based version.
7. 5 hashtags.
8. 1 carousel idea.
9. 1 image idea for Canva.
10. 1 question to end the post.

Rules:
- Make it sound human, not AI.
- Use short sentences.
- Add a clear opinion.
- Keep it professional.
- Do not exaggerate.
- Do not invent facts.
- Use Middle East context.
- Make it useful for professionals.
```

## 2. Rewrite a draft to be more viral

```text
Rewrite this LinkedIn post to make it stronger for professionals in Saudi Arabia and the Middle East.

Make it:
- More human
- More direct
- More opinionated
- Less generic
- Easy to read
- Focused on KSA/GCC opportunities
- Under 180 words

Use:
- A strong first line
- Short paragraphs
- Bullet points
- A clear takeaway
- A question at the end

Original draft:
[Paste draft]
```

## 3. Weekly roundup prompt

```text
Create a LinkedIn weekly roundup from these KSA/GCC updates.

Audience:
Professionals, founders, recruiters, and business owners in Saudi Arabia, UAE, and GCC.

Updates:
[Paste 5-8 links or summaries]

Output:
- One strong hook
- 5 concise updates
- One regional business takeaway
- One career/business implication
- 3-5 hashtags
- A final question

Tone:
Professional, practical, insightful, not hype.
```

## 4. Commenting prompt

```text
Create 5 thoughtful LinkedIn comments I can leave under a post about this topic:
[Paste topic or post summary]

Rules:
- Do not sound generic.
- Add one useful insight.
- Keep each comment under 45 words.
- Make it relevant to KSA/GCC professionals.
- Do not pitch my services.
```
